============= Commands PC => UD18

[reset Wh]    FF 55 11 03 01 00 00 00 00 51
[reset Ah]    FF 55 11 03 02 00 00 00 00 52
[reset Time]  FF 55 11 03 03 00 00 00 00 53
[reset All]   FF 55 11 03 05 00 00 00 00 5D
[Setup]       FF 55 11 03 31 00 00 00 00 01
[Enter]       FF 55 11 03 32 00 00 00 00 02
[+] Command   FF 55 11 03 33 00 00 00 00 03
[-] Command   FF 55 11 03 34 00 00 00 00 0C

============== ack-commands UD18 => PC

(OK reset Wh) FF 55 02 01 01 00 00 40
